About Us

The guy behind
the crew.

Doon Demolition is a Southern Alberta crew that shows up, works clean, and gets out of your way. Whether it's a single-room gut-out or a full structural teardown, the job gets done on schedule and you're not chasing us down for updates.

The owner, Jeff "Doon" Dunham, spent 10 years with the Princess Patricia's Canadian Light Infantry before starting the company. That background shapes how we run a job site: prepared, precise, and communicative.

Trusted by developers, general contractors, and homeowners across Calgary, Nanton, Okotoks, High River, Canmore, and surrounding communities.
